Electrical and System Engineering

Mission Statement:

The electircal and Systems Engineering department is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of sensing, imaging, telecommunications, control, security, and sustainable energy.

Background:

The second oldest electrical engineering department in the country, our faculty and students are dedicated to high quality, interdisciplinary and revolutionary education and research. The department has 330 undergraduate students, 220 master's students, 50 doctoral students, and 14 full-time faculty.
